Google tops the 2014 100 Top Brandz report by Millward Brown. Its value shot up 40% in a year to a huge £158.84 billion (£92.24 billion) Apple's value meanwhile fell by 20% to $147.88 billion (£87.74 billion) Millward Brown says this could be due to Google's investment in new tech. Highest riser was Chinese internet portal Tencent, which grew 97% to 14th.
